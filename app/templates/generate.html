Generate a Sitemap

To create a sitemap for your website, type or paste into the search field your URL, hit the 'Generate Sitemap' button. Then sit back and let Moore's Maps do the work to generate the sitemap for you.

Note: Make sure that you do not include the Https:// or Http:// protocol in your URL field.

Features

Scraping websites

Moore's maps allows you to scrape websites to gain a deeper understanding of them and to see different paths you can take through a website.

Viewing your results

When the scraping process is complete, you will see a graphical user interface, which you can expand and collapse in order to view different pages and it's child pages.

Downloading sections of your results

You can also download your results, simply input a name of a page you wish to see and hit download, you will recieve a PDF file of that pages immediate child pages, this can then be printed using the PDF viewer installed on your PC.
